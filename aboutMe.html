WHO IS THIS?

Caroline is a self-taught artist with a degree in architecture, interned for the government doing graphic design, and is currently transtitioning into front end development. She is driven by her insatiable need to improve and learn new things. With her versatile skillset, she seeks opportunities where she can contribute the full gamut of her capabilites, and learn a thing or two along the way.

Currently, Caroline is a student in General Assembly's Front End Web Developer course and is working hard to become a great developer! When not in class she lives in coffee shops where she codes, reads books about design, and sketches pictures of unsuspecting patrons.
